Portfolio Growth & Trading Desk Operations

ANALYTICS STRATEGY FINANCE

Problem

Investors needed consistent portfolio growth in a volatile market environment, requiring data-driven decision making to optimize trading strategies and minimize risk.

Action

  • Applied technical analysis techniques including RSI, Bollinger Bands, and Moving Averages to identify optimal entry and exit points
  • Built financial models to simulate different market scenarios and potential outcomes
  • Iterated trading strategies daily based on market conditions and performance data
  • Developed risk management frameworks to protect investor capital during market downturns

Result

  • Achieved 8% portfolio growth in challenging market conditions
  • Generated $3,000 profit on a $10,000 demo account
  • Facilitated 50%+ growth in investor capital under management
  • Developed repeatable frameworks for data-driven trading decisions

Reflection

This experience strengthened my ability to analyze complex data, test hypotheses, and iterate quickly — skills directly transferable to product experimentation and roadmap validation. The financial modeling expertise serves as a supporting strength for data-driven product decisions focused on market outcomes.
